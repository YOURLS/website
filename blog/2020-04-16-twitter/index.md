This is just a short announcement to highlight the fact that YOURLS online presence has been removed from Twitter. As a matter of fact, for completely unknown reasons, Twitter [has suspended](https://github.com/YOURLS/YOURLS/issues/2639) our @yourls and @yourls\_dev accounts (allegedly: breaking the terms of use, but despite my claims and requests I have no details and they just won't listen).

So, in you are on Twitter and find anything you read about YOURLS useful, be sure to share it. YOURLS "official" Twitter account being removed just means that, from now on, YOURLS Twitter accounts are its users'. Share the love and help your friends!

Stay safe. Virtual and social-distanced hugs from the YOURLS team.
